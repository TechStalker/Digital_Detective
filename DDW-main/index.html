How to use the Digital Detective website, navigate through it and access its features?

Here are the main features of our website , with the description of why and how to access each feature

01

Digital Detective Chatbot

Digital Detective Chatbot

This is Madhab School’s Digital Detective Chatbot that will guide you if you have basic technical and cyber issues. It will direct you to whom should you reach and what basic actions can you take . You can access this feature by clicking on the “ Get Started” Button .

02

Direct contact (Enquiry) with the school admin .

Direct contact (Enquiry) with the school admin .

In many cyber security situations that you might face using your school device you might need to contact and inform your school administration or the ESE SD team . You can inform your school with any kind of technical and cyber issues that you face by completing and sending the enquiry in the CONTACT tab on the navigation bar up .

03

The REACH OUT tab

The REACH OUT tab

In this tab we collected main resources and services offered by either UAE official authorities or global authorities , you might need when facing a technical or a cyber problem . You can access it by clicking on the REACH OUT tab on the navigation bar up and then selecting the type of problem you have .

04

The Digital Awareness Tab

The Digital Awareness Tab

In this page , we provided remarkable initiatives done by Emirati and Global Authorities, mentioning the recognizable efforts done by the Emirati Leadership to provide a safe cyber space .
